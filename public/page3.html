Some of Zlatan's most memorable quotes

  • "You Bought A Ferrari But You Drive It Like A Fiat"

    On his single season at Barcelona where he endured a tense relationship with then-coach Pep Guardiola.

  • "First I went left; he did too. Then I went right and he did too. Then I went left again and he went to buy a hot dog."

    On how he twisted Liverpool defender Stephane Henchoz inside-out.

  • "It felt like I had 11 babies around me."

    On Chelsea players' response to his red card in the 2015 Champions League quarterfinals.

  • "It was the fault of David Trezeguet, who made me do one drink of vodka after another. I slept in the bathtub. Now I hold my vodka much better."

    On having one too many after Juventus' 2005 title win.

  • "I came like a king, left like a legend."

    Zlatan on his PSG departure.

  • "I won't be the King of Manchester, I will be the God of Manchester."

    His response to Eric Cantona's comments that he would be the prince of Manchester.

  • "It’s been nice. You won’t win anything without me"

    Zlatan’s Inter Milan exit line.

  • “I don't think that you can score as spectacular a goal as those of Zlatan in a video game - even though these games are very realistic these days."

    On FIFA 16 and others are no match for Ibrahimovic.

  • “What Carew does with a football, I can do with an orange.”

    Ibrahimovic's response to Norway’s John Carew questioning the need for his tricks and flicks.

  • "I feel young. I said once before I'm like Benjamin Button."

    On joining LA Galaxy at age 36.
